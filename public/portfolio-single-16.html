    VICHY

  • Graphic Design, Merchandising
  • 2013
Vichy contracted us at Salad to design POS (point of sales) and POSM (point of sale materials) for their product Normaderm. I primarily used Photoshop and it's 3D features to impose the assets on the surfaces. My designs were used in kiosks and displays around the country.
  • Design Considerations
I began with print materials used in the U.S. and Europe, then brought in my own layouts and typography to match the new, larger medium.
